Mechanical Swine Facts

  • Pigs are highly intelligent animals and can learn tricks faster than dogs.
  • Pigs have an excellent sense of smell and are used to find truffles in Europe.
  • Despite the saying, pigs are actually very clean animals.
  • Pigs communicate with each other using grunts and squeals.
  • There are over 500 different breeds of pigs worldwide.
  • Baby pigs are called piglets, and a group of piglets is called a litter.
  • Pigs can run at speeds of up to 11 miles per hour.
  • Pigs' snouts are very sensitive and are used for rooting in the ground for food.
